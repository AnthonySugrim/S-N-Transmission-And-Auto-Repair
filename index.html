Qualified Full-Service Auto Repair Shop

Complete transmission diagnostics & repair

Your transmission is one of the most important — and complex — parts of your vehicle. At S & N Transmission and Auto Repair, we use advanced diagnostic tools to accurately pinpoint transmission issues and provide efficient, long-lasting repairs. Whether it’s slipping gears, delayed shifting, or fluid leaks, our experienced technicians will get your transmission back to factory performance.

General auto repairs and maintenance

From oil changes and tire rotations to check engine lights and battery replacements, we handle all your day-to-day maintenance needs. Our goal is to help Queens drivers stay safe and prevent small issues from turning into costly repairs. Regular maintenance keeps your car running smoothly and extends its lifespan — and we make it easy, honest, and affordable.

CVT issues, valve body replacements & more

Continuously Variable Transmissions (CVTs) are common in today’s vehicles — and require special expertise to service correctly. We handle all CVT-related problems, including valve body replacements, belt issues, and software updates. If your car feels jerky, unresponsive, or shows a transmission code, we’ve got the tools and experience to fix it right the first time.
